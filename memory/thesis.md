# Situational Awareness LP — Master Thesis Cheat Sheet

Source: Leopold Aschenbrenner's "Situational Awareness" essay + observed filings Q4 2024 to Q4 2025. The fund's edge is buying picks and shovels (power, fiber, miners, gas) rather than the obvious AI mega-caps (NVDA, MSFT, GOOG, META, AMZN).

When a NEW position appears in a 13F, look up the ticker here. If found, attach the matching thesis bucket. If not found, flag as off-thesis for human review (could signal a regime shift).

## Thesis buckets

1. **Power generation** (the core thesis). Every AI buildout needs ~100GW. Bet on capacity holders, on-site providers, gas upstream.
2. **Optics / fiber**. Interconnect bottleneck. Bet on optical leaders.
3. **Compute**. GPU rental scarcity. Bet on neoclouds and miners pivoting to HPC.
4. **Semiconductors**. Fab capacity returns from China-decoupling. Bet on Intel and alt foundries.
5. **Memory / storage**. Memory cycle catches AI tailwind.
6. **Natgas pickaxes**. Marcellus is the only realistic 100GW power path. Bet on producers and frac services.
7. **Real estate**. Datacenter buildout REITs.
8. **Shorts on disrupted businesses**. IT outsourcing consultancies (puts).
